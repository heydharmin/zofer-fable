What to take from each

Pattern extraction

Scheduled & choose-a-pro

JustLife · Urban Company
  • Configure → slot → choose pro is the proven scheduled path — maps 1:1 to chauffeur.
  • Upfront ‘from AED’ package price before any commitment.
  • Same-pro recurring is the retention engine — rebook the person you liked.

Map-first, on-demand

Lime · Dott
  • Map is the home — see supply + ETA instantly; near-zero taps to start.
  • Reserve-ahead (Dott) holds a vehicle — the analog of holding a chauffeur slot.
  • End with a park photo — proof-of-state at handoff (→ our odometer/fuel photo + PIN).

Super-app shell

Smiles
  • Service grid home with easy vertical switching.
  • Wallet + points redeemed at checkout — our Rafeeq Plus / AED wallet.
  • Earn on every action — loyalty loop that pulls the next booking.

The chauffeur twist

unique to us
  • Bring-your-own-car — car-first home (like the scooter map, but the car is the object).
  • Choose-your-chauffeur gated by urgency (on-demand = auto-match; scheduled = choose).
  • Key-handover trust — PIN + insurance where scooters only need a park photo.
