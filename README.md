# Chauffeur Super-App — UX Case Study 🚗

Redesigning a UAE bring-your-own-car chauffeur super-app around one idea:

> **Careem assigns you a stranger. We introduce you to YOUR chauffeur** — a named, rated,
> rebookable driver who becomes *your regular* across school runs, airport, and nights out.

Produced with the [**UX Council**](https://github.com/heydharmin/ux-council) multi-agent method —
**4 sprints, 197 AI design agents**, benchmarked against Careem, Urban Company, JustLife & Uber Reserve.

## The north star

**Repeat-driver rebooking rate** — the funnel every screen builds:
`one-off → favorite → regular → subscription`.

Open `prototype.html` in a browser and tap through it — that clip is the portfolio piece.
